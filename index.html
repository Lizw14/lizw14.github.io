Biography

I am a 4th-year PhD student at Johns Hopkins University, supervised by Prof. Alan Yuille and Prof. Benjamin Van Durme. I am a member of CCVL lab. Before this, I received my B.E. degree at Tsinghua Univeristy in 2018. I have also spent wonderful times at Facebook, Adobe and Sensetime.

My research interest includes computer vision and natural language processing.

Biography

I am a 2nd-year PhD student at Johns Hopkins University, supervised by Prof. Alan Yuille. I am a member of CCVL lab. Before this, I received my B.E. degree at Tsinghua Univeristy. I have also spent wonderful times at Adobe and Sensetime.

My research interest includes computer vision and natural language processing.
